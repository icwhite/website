Hi! I am first-year PhD Student at UCSD advised by Prithviraj Ammanabrolu and Lianhui Qin. Previously I did my undergrad at UC Berkeley in Computer Science and was advised by Sergey Levine.


Within the intersection of Natural Language Processing and Reinforcement Learning I am interested in the following topics:

  • Human-language agent interaction I am excited about agents that learn through interaction to collaborate with humans, by being honest and helpful.
  • Commonsense Reasoning
Beyond this, I am interested in developing more efficient open-source models and developing models that can have real-world impact. Reach out if you are interested in collaborating!
